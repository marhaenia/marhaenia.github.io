A little about Marhaenia

You might be wondering what is the Socialist Republic of Marhaenia. The Socialist Republic of Marhaenia, commonly known by its short name, Marhaenia, is an entity that claimed to be sovereign state, but commonly known as a micronation. It is comprised of 8 provinces which is bordered to the macronation of the Unitary State of the Republic of Indonesia, and was declared independence on March 13, 2023.
