Treaty of Mutual Recognition between the Socialist Republic of Marhaenia and the Republic of Alrodnia

The Government of the Socialist Republic of Marhaenia and the Government of the Republic of Alrodnia, hereinafter referred to as "the Parties,"

RECOGNIZING the principles of mutual respect, self-determination, and cooperation among nations,

DESIROUS of establishing diplomatic relations and fostering friendship between their respective nations,

ACKNOWLEDGING the unique circumstances and aspirations of nations and their contributions to the global community,

HAVE AGREED as follows:

 

Article 1: Establishment of Diplomatic Relations

1. The Parties shall establish diplomatic relations at an appropriate level, acknowledging each other as sovereign and independent nations.

2. The Parties shall exchange official representatives to facilitate communication and cooperation between their nations.

 

Article 2: Mutual Recognition

1. The Parties mutually recognize each other as legitimate and autonomous nations, with full respect for their sovereignty, territorial integrity, and internal affairs.

2. The Parties shall respect and promote the rights and freedoms of their respective citizens within the framework of international law and applicable treaties.

 

Article 3: Cooperation and Exchanges

1. The Parties shall encourage cooperation in various areas of mutual interest, such as culture, arts, education, technology, sport, and environmental conservation, based on their respective capabilities and interests.

2. The Parties shall promote exchanges between their citizens, including visits, cultural events, and collaborations, to foster understanding and friendship among their nations.

 

Article 4: Consular Relations

1. The Parties may consider establishing consular relations or appropriate mechanisms to facilitate consular services and protect the rights and interests of their citizens within the scope of their respective nations.

2. The Parties may provide assistance to each other's citizens in need, subject to the limitations and capabilities of their nations.

 

Article 5: Settlement of Disputes

1. Any disputes arising from the interpretation or application of this Treaty shall be resolved amicably through direct negotiations between the Parties.

2. If the Parties fail to reach a mutually acceptable solution through negotiations, they may explore alternative means of peaceful dispute resolution.

 

Article 6: Termination

1. This Treaty shall remain in force indefinitely unless terminated by mutual agreement between the Parties or if either nation ceases to exist or significantly alters its status.

2. Either Party may terminate this Treaty by providing written notice to the other Party. Termination shall become effective 30 days after the receipt of such notice.

 

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the undersigned, being duly authorized by their respective nations, have signed this Treaty.

DONE in duplicate, in the English language, on 9 July 2023, in Apisia, Alrodnia.

 

Signed,

 

A.M. Marhaen,

President of the Socialist Republic of Marhaenia

 

Syarif I.M.

Prime Minister of the Republic of Alrodnia
